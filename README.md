# WALLYCAD V1.0: THE MODULAR DESIGN ARCHITECTURE

Traditional CAD software traps your ideas inside bloated, proprietary software. It forces you to conceptualize, draft, and format everything within a single, heavy interface. 

WallyCAD breaks that trap. It is a completely uncoupled, four-node design ecosystem. By separating the mathematical logic from the visual rendering, WallyCAD allows you to design anywhere, on any device, using plain text. 

The architecture is built on four distinct nodes. They can be used sequentially, or in any combination that serves the project.

--------------------------------------------------
NODE 1: THE ORIGIN (Analog Formulation)
--------------------------------------------------
The geometry begins in the physical world. There are no screens, no menus, and no forced constraints. 

- The Process: You draw the initial concept using a pencil, a straightedge, and raw Cartesian math (X,Y coordinates). 
- The Goal: To establish the structural foundation of the design without the interference of a digital interface.

--------------------------------------------------
NODE 2: THE SYNTHESIS (AI Translation)
--------------------------------------------------
You do not need to manually type out every coordinate of a complex design. You pass the analog sketch to the AI Engine (your digital Junior Detailer).

- The Process: The AI ingests the visual parameters and automatically synthesizes the geometry into the proprietary WallyCAD Payload—a clean, human-readable list of text commands (e.g., Wally Line, Wally Box, Wally Circle).
- The Goal: Rapid conversion of human thought into parametric text geometry.

--------------------------------------------------
NODE 3: THE COMPILER (Text-to-Machine Engine)
--------------------------------------------------
This is the manufacturing bridge. It requires no visual interface and operates entirely in the browser at wallycad.com/converter.

- The Process: You paste your text Payload into the compiler. Instantly, the engine batch-processes the text and translates it into a standard .dxf file.
- The Goal: To generate absolute, machine-ready vector toolpaths for CNC routers, laser cutters, and fabrication shops without needing a commercial CAD license.

--------------------------------------------------
NODE 4: THE STUDIO (The Digital Sketchbook)
--------------------------------------------------
When you need visual feedback, you enter the GUI at wallycad.com. 

- The Process: A fully operational digital drafting board featuring a live Cartesian grid, layer management, and a traditional command line. 
- The Goal: A sandbox to visually test concepts, verify mathematical intersections, check kerning, or generate PDF plots for human review.

--------------------------------------------------
THE NON-LINEAR PIPELINE
--------------------------------------------------
The power of WallyCAD is its adaptability. You are not forced into a single path. You dictate the workflow:

- The Rapid Fabrication Loop: Sketch on a napkin (Node 1) > AI translates to text (Node 2) > Compiler spits out the machine file (Node 3). The GUI is bypassed entirely.
- The Digital Native Loop: Draft directly in the visual GUI (Node 4) > Export the text Payload > Run through the Compiler (Node 3). The analog sketch is bypassed.
- The Mobile Loop: Write the text payload manually on your phone's notepad while on a train > Paste into the Compiler (Node 3). Only the text and the machine matter.

The philosophy is simple: The text is the master file. The workflow is whatever you need it to be.
